About the Tutor

I help students build strong foundations in Python programming, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. My teaching style blends clear explanations, hands-on coding, and real-world applications so that you not only learn concepts but also understand how to apply them in projects and problem-solving scenarios.

Skills & Focus

  • Python: Syntax, OOP, problem solving
  • Data Science: Pandas, NumPy, data cleaning & visualization
  • AI & ML: Neural Networks, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch basics
  • Hands-on practical projects

Approach

My approach is to make learning Python for AI & Data Science simple, structured, and engaging. Every session includes theory, coding exercises, and discussions on real-world use cases. Students work on mini-projects and weekly assessments to strengthen problem-solving skills and gain confidence for academic exams as well as practical usage of their skills.

What You’ll Gain

By the end of this journey, you'll be comfortable with Python programming and its applications Data science field and get exposure to AI. You’ll have experience with datasets, building simple machine learning models, and visualizing insights — all while improving your logical thinking and coding efficiency.
